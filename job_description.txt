About The Job

We are seeking a Senior Program Manager to lead strategic initiatives within Saxo’s Foundation Portfolio, focused on simplifying the core, strengthening data and platforms, and embedding automation and AI to enable scalable, efficient, and compliant growth.

This is a high-impact leadership role at the intersection of strategy and execution, requiring mastery in driving large-scale transformation, orchestrating complexity, and delivering measurable business outcomes.

You will join the Group PMO, a central new established function driving strategy execution excellence, portfolio transparency, and value realization, with direct exposure to senior leadership and enterprise-wide change.

Key Responsibilities

Lead end-to-end transformation programs. Drive large-scale, cross-functional initiatives from strategy through execution, ensuring clear scope, disciplined delivery, and outcome focus
Translate strategy into measurable impact. Convert strategic priorities into structured roadmaps, OKRs, and delivery plans, ensuring tangible business outcomes are monitored
Orchestrate enterprise alignment: Align business, technology, data, and operations, managing dependencies, sequencing, and trade-offs across initiatives
Ensure delivery discipline: Own plans, timelines, budgets, and resources, ensuring execution within scope, capacity, and regulatory constraints
Drive coordinated execution across multiple parallel initiatives, managing trade-offs and interdependencies to deliver integrated outcomes
Manage risk and complexity: Proactively identify and mitigate risks and interdependencies, ensuring transparency to senior stakeholders
Engage senior stakeholders: Act as a trusted partner to leadership, providing clear reporting, governance, and decision support
Drive adoption and value realization: Ensure new capabilities are embedded and adopted, delivering sustained organisational impact
Drive continuous improvement and PM excellence Contribute to strengthening PMO standards, tools, and ways of working

Your profile

We are looking for an ambitious, structured, and impact-driven leader who is motivated by solving complex challenges and delivering tangible business outcomes.

You bring: 8–10+ years of experience delivering large-scale transformation programmes with enterprise-wide impact
A strong track record within financial services and/or complex technology environments
Proven ability to translate strategic direction into concrete execution and measurable results
Extensive experience working in cross-functional, international organisations
Solid expertise in programme governance, risk management, and structured delivery frameworks (e.g. PMI, Agile, or hybrid models)
Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills, with the ability to influence and engage senior leadership
A highly structured, analytical, and outcome-oriented mindset, combined with strong ownership and problem-solving capabilities
